Capital High Science Camps

These are camps put on by students at Capital High School. Our goal is to educate the future scientists, doctors, and programmers by giving kids first hand experience in STEM and by showing kids that science is fun! The camps cost $45 per child. The proceeds go directly to the Capital High Science Club, and they are used to fund more science camps and our own scientific adventures.

Courses

Stars and Beyond

Grades: 2-4

Dates Offered: June 25-27

Description: In this camp, kids will get the chance to learn about planets, stars, black holes, and everything in between. Through interesting videos and fun models, students will gain a better understanding of space and astronomy.

Exploring the Human Body

Grades: 4-6

Dates Offered: June 25-27

Description: Students will participate in games, labs, and fun, hands-on activities while learning more about the human body and the incredible things it’s capable of.

Constructing Creatively

Grades: 1-3

Dates Offered: June 25-27

Description: This camp is for anyone who loves using their hands and building things. Some of the activities include building an earthquake tower and creating catapults. *Please note this is the same curriculum as in previous camps.

Chemistry

Grades: 4-6

Dates Offered: July 9-11

Description: Slime, explosions, and awesome chemical reactions! This camp gives kids the opportunity to participate in fun, interactive chemistry labs. *Please note this is the same curriculum as in previous camps.

Earth Science

Grades: 1-3

Dates Offered: July 9-11

Description: Learn about some of Earth’s most destructive natural disasters such as earthquakes, volcanoes, and floods, look at cool fossils and rocks, and learn about the weather

Scratch Programming

Grades: 2-4

Dates Offered: July 9-11

Description: Create exciting stories, games and animations while learning the basic principles of coding with an easy to use program,: Scratch!. *Please note this is the same curriculum as in previous camps.

Robotics

Grades: 2-3

Dates Offered: July 30 - August 1

Description: Gain an understanding of Lego robotics. Learn how to build and program a robot to interact with the surrounding world. *Please note this is the same curriculum as in previous camps.

Harry Potter

Grades: 2-4

Dates Offered: July 30 - August 1

Description: Haven’t received your Hogwarts acceptance letter yet? At this camp kids will get to experience the world of Harry Potter, taking potions, herbology, and Magical Creatures classes. In this fun Harry Potter themed camp kids will learn different chemical reactions, as well as unique plants and animals.

The Amazing World of Science

Grades: 4-6

Dates Offered: July 30 - August 1

Description: Kids will participate in a number of fun labs, including making slime, bouncy balls, and a contraption to prevent an egg from breaking. *Note this camp is a combination of all the activities previously done in the Natural Science and General Science camps.
