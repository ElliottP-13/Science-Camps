Capital High Science Camps

These are camps put on by students at Capital High School. Our goal is to educate the future scientists, doctors, and programmers by giving kids first hand experience in STEM and by showing kids that science is fun! The camps cost $20 for the first child, then $15 for each additional child from the same family. The proceeds go directly to the Capital High Science Club, and they are used to fund more science camps and our own scientific adventures.
