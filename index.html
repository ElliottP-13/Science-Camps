Capital High Science Camps

These are camps put on by students at Capital High School. Our goal is to educate the future scientists, doctors, and programmers by giving kids first hand experience in STEM and by showing kids that science is fun! The camps cost $45 for three days. The procedes go directly to the Capital High Science Club, and they are used to fund more science camps and our own scientific adventures.

Courses

  • Scratch Programing - Grades 2-6 - Dates July 19-21 - Learn the basics of programming through the fun and interactive program of scratch
  • Exploratory Biology - Grades 1-5 - Dates July 19-21 - Have fun learning basic biology concepts from evolution to disections
  • Intro to Robotics - Grades 1-5 - Dates Jun 27-29, Aug 1-3 - Gain an understanding of Lego Robotics. Learn how to build, and program a robot using sensors to accomplish a task
  • Forensics - Grades 3-7 - Dates Jun 27-29, Aug 1-3 - Learn the basics of Chemistry and Forensics while solving a murder mystery...
  • Intro to Code - Grades 4-8 - Dates July 19-21 - Learn the basics of HTML to create your own website!
  • Exploratory Physics - Grades 4-8 - Dates Jun 27-29 - Understand the world around you through physics! Students will gain a broad knowlege of physics from Newton's laws and mechanical physics to understanding the waves of light
  • Natural Science - Grades 4-8 - Dates Aug 1-3 - Gain a broad understanding of the world around you. Understand the rudementary concepts of physics, biology, chemistry, and earth science!
